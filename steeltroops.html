Doraemon: Nobita and the New Steel Troops

Audio: Hindi

Description

Doraemon: Nobita and the Steel Troops is a 1986 Japanese animated science fiction action-adventure film based on the seventh volume of the same name of the Doraemon Long Stories series. The original plot was written by Fujiko F. Fujio. Alternate titles include The Platoon of Iron Men, or The Robot Army.
