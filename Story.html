Story

The story revolves around an earless robotic cat named Doraemon, who travels back in time from the 22nd century to aid a boy named Nobita Nobi. Nobita Nobi is a lazy and coward boy who is not got at study's and sports. Sewashi, Nobita's great-great-grandson in 22nd, sends Doraemon to help Nobita Nobi. The manga spawned a media franchise. Three anime TV series have been adapted in 1973, 1979, and 2005.
